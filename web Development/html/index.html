JEE Main Counselling 2023 - JoSAA will conduct the JEE Main 2023 counselling process in online mode.

  • Candidates qualified on the basis of NTA JEE 2023 results will be eligible to apply for the counselling of JEE Main 2023
  • In order to get admission in NITs, IIITs or GFTIs. The JoSAA authorities will conduct the counselling for JEE Main in 6 rounds.
  • The Joint Seat Allocation Authority will start the JoSAA registration and choice filling 2023 process on June 19, 2023. Interested candidates will have to complete the JoSAA 2023 registration process within specified dates.
  • Only registered aspirants will be eligible to enroll in the counselling of JEE Main 2023. JEE Main counselling 2023 through JoSAA includes steps such as registration.
  • Choice filling, locking of choices, seat allotment, and payment of seat acceptance fee.
JoSAA will also conduct two rounds of mock seat allotment of JEE Mains before the actual rounds of JEE Main counselling 2023 to help students understand the process.

JEE Main Counselling 2023 Highlights

  • JoSAA organises a combined counselling process for admission of JEE Main and JEE Advanced qualified candidates in IITs, NITs, IIITs and GFTIs.
  • The counselling for vacant seats after JoSAA counselling will be organised by Central Seat Allocation Board (CSAB)
  • Supernumerary seats in colleges of the North East and Union Territories locations are allocated by special counselling CSAB NEUT
  • Central Seat Allocation Board will also conduct a separate counselling procedure CSAB SFTI for 18 Self Financing Technical Institutes (SFTIs)
